Pain Relief & Anti-inflammatory
Aspirin (Acetylsalicylic Acid)
Chemical Formula: C₉H₈O₄
Molecular Weight: 180.16 g/mol
Structure: Salicylate derivative with acetyl group

✅ Benefits

  • Effective pain relief
  • Anti-inflammatory properties
  • Reduces fever
  • Cardiovascular protection
  • Widely available and affordable

❌ Side Effects

  • Stomach irritation
  • Bleeding risk
  • Not suitable for children
  • Drug interactions
  • Allergic reactions possible

🌿 Natural Alternative: Willow Bark

Contains salicin, which converts to salicylic acid in the body. Used traditionally for pain and inflammation. Less potent than aspirin but gentler on the stomach.

Ibuprofen (Advil, Motrin)
Chemical Formula: C₁₃H₁₈O₂
Molecular Weight: 206.28 g/mol
Structure: Propionic acid derivative (NSAID)

✅ Benefits

  • Strong anti-inflammatory
  • Effective pain relief
  • Fever reduction
  • Less stomach irritation than aspirin
  • Available over-the-counter

❌ Side Effects

  • Kidney damage risk
  • Cardiovascular risks
  • GI side effects
  • Not suitable during pregnancy
  • Drug interactions

🌿 Natural Alternative: Turmeric

Contains curcumin, a powerful anti-inflammatory compound. Studies show it can be as effective as ibuprofen for some types of pain without the side effects.

Acetaminophen (Tylenol)
Chemical Formula: C₈H₉NO₂
Molecular Weight: 151.16 g/mol
Structure: Para-aminophenol derivative

✅ Benefits

  • Effective pain relief
  • Fever reduction
  • Gentle on stomach
  • Safe for children
  • No blood thinning effects

❌ Side Effects

  • Liver damage risk
  • No anti-inflammatory effects
  • Easy to overdose
  • Interactions with alcohol
  • Hidden in many medications

🌿 Natural Alternative: Ginger

Contains gingerols and shogaols with pain-relieving properties. Particularly effective for headaches and muscle pain. Also helps with nausea.

Naproxen (Aleve)
Chemical Formula: C₁₄H₁₄O₃
Molecular Weight: 230.26 g/mol
Structure: Propionic acid derivative

✅ Benefits

  • Long-lasting pain relief
  • Strong anti-inflammatory
  • Effective for chronic pain
  • Fewer doses needed

❌ Side Effects

  • Cardiovascular risks
  • Stomach irritation
  • Kidney problems
  • Slower onset of action

🌿 Natural Alternative: Boswellia

Also known as Indian frankincense, contains boswellic acids that reduce inflammation. Particularly effective for arthritis and chronic pain conditions.

Tramadol
Chemical Formula: C₁₆H₂₅NO₂
Molecular Weight: 263.38 g/mol
Structure: Synthetic opioid analgesic

✅ Benefits

  • Effective for moderate pain
  • Lower abuse potential than other opioids
  • Dual mechanism of action
  • Available in extended-release forms

❌ Side Effects

  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Dizziness and drowsiness
  • Constipation
  • Seizure risk
  • Dependence potential

🌿 Natural Alternative: Kratom

Contains mitragynine and 7-hydroxymitragynine with pain-relieving properties. However, use with caution as it can be habit-forming and legal status varies.

Heart & Blood Pressure
Lisinopril (Zestril)
Chemical Formula: C₂₁H₃₁N₃O₅
Molecular Weight: 405.5 g/mol
Structure: ACE inhibitor

✅ Benefits

  • Lowers blood pressure
  • Protects heart and kidneys
  • Reduces stroke risk
  • Once-daily dosing

❌ Side Effects

  • Dry cough
  • Hyperkalemia
  • Angioedema (rare)
  • Kidney problems

🌿 Natural Alternative: Hawthorn

Contains oligomeric procyanidins that support heart health and may help lower blood pressure. Often combined with garlic and CoQ10 for cardiovascular support.

Metoprolol (Lopressor)
Chemical Formula: C₁₅H₂₅NO₃
Molecular Weight: 267.36 g/mol
Structure: Beta-blocker

✅ Benefits

  • Reduces heart rate
  • Lowers blood pressure
  • Prevents heart attacks
  • Reduces anxiety symptoms

❌ Side Effects

  • Fatigue and dizziness
  • Cold hands and feet
  • Depression
  • Bronchospasm

🌿 Natural Alternative: Magnesium

Magnesium glycinate helps relax blood vessels and can lower blood pressure. Also supports heart rhythm and reduces stress-related heart palpitations.

Simvastatin (Zocor)

Molecular Weight: 418.57 g/mol
Structure: HMG-CoA reductase inhibitor

✅ Benefits

  • Lowers cholesterol
  • Reduces heart disease risk
  • Prevents strokes
  • Well-studied efficacy

  • Liver problems
  • Memory issues
  • Diabetes risk

🌿 Natural Alternative: Red Yeast Rice

Contains naturally occurring statins (monacolin K). Combined with CoQ10 and omega-3 fatty acids, it can help lower cholesterol naturally.

Warfarin (Coumadin)
Chemical Formula: C₁₉H₁₆O₄
Molecular Weight: 308.33 g/mol
Structure: Vitamin K antagonist

✅ Benefits

  • Prevents blood clots
  • Reduces stroke risk
  • Treats atrial fibrillation
  • Well-established use

❌ Side Effects

  • Bleeding risk
  • Requires regular monitoring
  • Food interactions
  • Drug interactions

🌿 Natural Alternative: Ginkgo Biloba

Improves circulation and has mild blood-thinning properties. Should be used with caution and medical supervision, especially with other blood thinners.

Diabetes Management

Chemical Formula: C₄H₁₁N₅
Molecular Weight: 129.16 g/mol
Structure: Biguanide

✅ Benefits

  • Lowers blood glucose
  • Weight loss friendly
  • Cardiovascular benefits
  • First-line diabetes treatment

❌ Side Effects

  • Metallic taste

🌿 Natural Alternative: Berberine

Alkaloid found in goldenseal and barberry. Studies show it can lower blood sugar as effectively as metformin by improving insulin sensitivity.

Ceylon cinnamon contains compounds that improve insulin sensitivity and glucose metabolism.

  • Used for atypical bacteria
